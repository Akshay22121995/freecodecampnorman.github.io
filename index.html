What is freeCodeCamp.org?

FreeCodeCamp.org is a supportive group of over one million coders who build projects, earn free certificates, and get experience by coding for non-profits. Go to freeCodeCamp.org to sign up for a free account and start learning with through the curriculum.

About Us

We're a group focused on learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and web development using the freeCodeCamp curriculum. Everyone is welcome, from beginners looking to get started to more experienced coders looking to help others while improving their own skills. Join us and introduce yourself and meet others who are also learning to code. Create a free account on freeCodeCamp.org and bring your laptop if you have one! If you don't have one, message one of the organizers on Meetup.com and we will try and prepare one for you.

Meetups

We meet every Tuesday evening at 6:30pm at the Tom Love Innovation Hub on the South Research Campus of the University of Oklahoma. Even though the meetups are hosted on campus, we're open to everyone! Don't worry if you can't make it at 6:30 on the dot, or if you can't stay the whole two hours. Feel free to join us when you can and for as long as you want.
